In music notation, a Bar represents a segment of time corrosponding to a spacific number of beats that are are closed off by vertical boundries called bar line

Similar to clefs, the Time signiture establishes the rhythmic context of a particular piece of music.

The top figure represents the amount of beats present in any of the following bars, whereas the bottom figure determines the note value of those beats.

The example above shows a 4/4 time signiture also known as the common-time. The number 4 above indicates 4 beats in a bar with a note value of crotchet.
