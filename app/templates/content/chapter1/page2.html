CLEFS

In western music notation, notes reside either in one of 4 spaces or 5 horrizontal lines of a stave. But inorder to place a note with it's intended meaning, some context must be established on a given stave.

A clef determines which note a particular line/space will represent. The trebel 𝄞 and bass 𝄢 clef were two of the most commonly used in music notation.
