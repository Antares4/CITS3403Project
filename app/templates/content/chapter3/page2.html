What is a key

A Key represents a group of core pitches used in a piece of music where melody, chords, and bassline are all derived from.

  1. This group features a center note called the Tonic and its 6 other corrosponding notes
  2. Keys are named after their tonic note and belong to either the major or minor category
  3. A scale displays this group of notes in sequence
